📜 Rules & Roles
👑 Role Hierarchy
owner
Owner 👑
Full control. Can ban anyone, promote/demote all ranks, delete any content, post announcements, and manage the entire platform.
admin
Admin 🔴
Can ban Members and Mods. Can promote Members to Mod. Can delete any content, post announcements, and manage reports. Cannot ban other Admins or the Owner.
mod
Moderator 🔵
Can ban Members only. Can delete Members' messages and report content. Cannot ban Mods, Admins, or the Owner. Cannot post announcements.
member
Member ⚫
Default rank. Can upload clips, chat, comment, like, follow, and use all standard features. Can only delete their own messages and content.
📋 Community Rules
1.Respect everyone — No harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or bullying of any kind. Treat others how you want to be treated.
2.No spam — Don't flood chat with repeated messages, don't upload duplicate clips, and don't advertise other platforms.
3.Keep it gaming — Only upload clips, screenshots, and highlights related to gaming content.
4.No cheating content — Don't upload clips that show hacking, cheating, exploiting bugs, or anything that ruins others' gameplay.
5.Keep it clean — No NSFW, sexual, graphic, or violent content of any kind. ArenaClips is for all ages.
6.No doxing or threats — Never share anyone's personal information or make threats of any kind. Instant permanent ban.
7.Respect copyright — Only upload content you created or have permission to share. Don't steal other creators' clips.
8.No impersonation — Don't pretend to be another user, streamer, or staff member.
9.Use English in Global Chat — Keep the main chat in English so everyone can understand. Game chats can use any language.
10.Respect staff decisions — If a Mod or Admin warns you, listen. Arguing with staff publicly is not allowed. Use reports to appeal.
💬 Chat Rules
•Messages are visible for 24 hours then automatically removed
•One message every 1.5 seconds — no rapid-fire spam
•Max 500 characters per message
•Staff Yaps is for Mods, Admins, and Owner only
•Don't beg for roles in chat — use the Role Application below
🚫 Ban Policy
•1st offence: Warning in chat or DM from staff
•2nd offence: 1–3 day temporary ban
•3rd offence: 1 week–1 month ban
•Severe violations (doxing, threats, NSFW): Instant permanent ban, no appeal
•Bans can be appealed by contacting the Owner directly via DM
📤 Upload Rules
•Max file size: 500MB per clip
•Clips are saved as private Drafts first — you choose when to post publicly
•Don't post the same clip multiple times
•Add proper tags so others can find your clips
•All uploads are subject to review by staff
⚖️ Terms of Service
•By using ArenaClips you agree to all rules above
•You are fully responsible for all content you upload
•We reserve the right to remove content or ban users for any rule violation
•Your data is stored securely via Google Firebase — we never sell your data
•ArenaClips is in BETA — features may change at any time without notice
